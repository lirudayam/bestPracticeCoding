In comparison to variables, constants shall be used for maintaining a single source of truth of a value within the code. Usually they are not editable. It's common practice to write constants in CAPSLOCK.
Understanding that not every stored peace of data is done for modification, you can avoid errors during runtime. They enhance the idea of read-only data.
<br /><br />
Mostly these are been defined using keywords like e.g. "const" or "final". It may still be necessary to define the data type of constant.
